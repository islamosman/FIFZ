how to ride

FIND & UNLOCK

Find a Rabbit near you
and scan to unlock

SAFE RIDING

Bring your own helmet
to stay safe while
you ride

START RABBIT

Push with your foot to
get going - user throttle
button to accelerate

FOOT PLACEMENT

Place both feet on
footboard while riding

BRAKES

Press on brakes handle
with left hand to slow
down

END RIDE

End your ride by
tapping the button
within the app
